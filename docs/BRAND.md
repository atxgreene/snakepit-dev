# Snakepit Brand System

## Positioning

Snakepit is not a generic AI agency. It is a technical-forward AI operations consultancy for teams that need to reduce token burn, govern AI agents, and fix broken workflows.

## Personality

- Technical
- Dangerous but premium
- Precise
- Operator-first
- Anti-hype
- Command-center aesthetic

## Core line

Bring your AI stack under control.

## Supporting lines

- Stop token burn. Fix agent chaos. Ship useful AI.
- AI systems that are cheaper, safer, observable, and owned.
- Most AI failures are workflow ownership failures.

## Logo direction

Coiled snake forming an `S` around a terminal/agent symbol. The mark should work as a favicon and as a small badge in a command-center style dashboard.
